About

Renewal for homes, and for the people who restore them

Second Wind Exteriors was built on the belief that excellent work can come from people who simply needed a fair opportunity. We deliver premium roof and gutter cleaning throughout Sonoma County while creating meaningful employment for those who have been ignored, overlooked, or cast aside.

Mission statement

Second Wind Exteriors believes in renewal—for homes, and for the people who restore them. We offer work opportunities to capable individuals who have faced barriers, proving that discipline, craftsmanship, and second chances still matter.
